How to order:

To order a commission you can either contact me by DM's on twitter or discord (@juangames), or you can send me an email to my adress at juangames@duck.com with the following information:


Email subject: Commission

Your name: (Doesn't have to be your real name, only how you want to be refered as)

Type of commission: (Either flat colors, normal or full)

Email: (the one you have linked into your paypal account)

Description of the commission:

Regarding the description, you must send references of the character, along with the pose you want.

Once you've sent the message (either email or dm) I will answer ASAP, usually no longer than 2 days, telling you if I accepted or declined the commission.

Workflow:

When I accept the commission you must pay upfront, I will request you money through paypal. Once I recieve the money I will start working on it and notify you about this. Do not send any payment before I give you a slot and actually ask you for it.

I will be sending you multiple WIPs so you can see how the commission is going and ask for changes in time so it's just right for you.

The time to finish it may vary depending on personal circunstances, health, the complexity of the drawing etc.

Once the drawing is finished I will send you the full-resolution image and, if it's the case, without the background through an email.

In the rare case of the files size being too big for an email, I will send a google drive link containing these files, which may be deleted over time, so please download it ASAP.

If you're interested I can send the .psd file along with the images if you want.
